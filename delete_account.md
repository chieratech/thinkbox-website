# Delete Your ThinkBox Account

**App Name:** ThinkBox
**Developer:** Chiera Technologies

---

## 🗑️ How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your ThinkBox account directly داخل التطبيق (within the app) by following these steps:

1. Open the **ThinkBox App**
2. Go to the **Home Screen**
3. Tap on **Profile**
4. Navigate to **Privacy & Settings**
5. Tap on **Delete Account**
6. Confirm your request

---

## 🔗 Request Account Deletion (Alternative Method)

## 📂 What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

* Your profile information (name, email, profile photo)
* All ThinkBoxes
* All saved ideas (text and voice notes)
* Tags and organization data
* Any uploaded files or attachments

---

## ⚠️ Data That May Be Retained

We may retain certain limited data for legal, security, or operational purposes:

* Transaction records (if applicable)
* Logs for fraud prevention and security
* Backup data (automatically deleted within **30 days**)

This retained data will not be used for any commercial purpose.

---

## ⏳ Retention Period

* Immediate deletion: Most user data is deleted فوراً (immediately) after confirmation
* Backup systems: Data may remain for up to **30 days** before permanent deletion

---

## 🔒 Important Notes

* Account deletion is **permanent and cannot be undone**
* Once deleted, your ideas and data cannot be recovered
* Make sure to export any important information before deleting your account
